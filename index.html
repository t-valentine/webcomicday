What is this?

Webcomics (to me) are heavily DIY- artists and writers choosing to take stories into their own hands and publishing it for anyone who is interested (often for free). Say something nice about how an RSS feeds, explain how they're used. Make a comment about social media burying artists.

There's a lot of great RSS Feed Reader extensions you can download for your browser, but in the DIY spirit- here is an RSS Feed Reader extension, you create, you edit, and that you can customize to your heart's content. Did you know that browser extensions can be made using good old HTML and CSS? Yeah! You can customize your own RSS Feed Reader! Easily!
